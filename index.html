Yatzy score

Play Store Github app Github server

A simple Yatzy score app with real time score sharing and score history.

5 dices and this app. That is all you need to play Yatzy with your friends.
This app currently supports the rules of Yatzy and Yahtzee with an option for Yahtzee bonus.
* View the score of other players nearby without setup and account
* Score validation
* Overview of the total scores of your played games
* Statistics of your played games
* Dark mode
